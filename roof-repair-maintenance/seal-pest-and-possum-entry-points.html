Sealing entry/exit points is a vital part of getting pests and possums out of your roof cavity and must be done BEFORE trapping. Professional repairs and quality materials must be used to keep pests out.

TP Roofing are experts at identifying and sealing pest and possum entry points on your roof. We secure down tiles and ridge caps that can be lifted by our sneaky marsupials, seal cracks and crevices using metal or steel mesh/screen, custom made flashings, screws, quality sealants, chimney caps and metal vent covers, choosing the best products to repair your roof and possum proof your home.
