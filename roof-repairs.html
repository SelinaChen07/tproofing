TP Roofing thoroughly assess your roof problems and provide roof repairs and roof maintenance to tile, metal, slate and polycarbonate roofs. You receive prompt, reliable, personal service and roofing solutions at an affordable price by our licenced roof tilers and roof plumbers.

Roofing repair and regular roof maintenance extend the life of your existing roof.

Roof repair services include:

  • TILE ROOF REPAIR
  • SLATE ROOF REPAIR
  • METAL ROOF REPAIR
  • POLYCARBONATE ROOF

Regular clearing of debris from roof, gutters and downpipes prevents blockages. Buildup of debris can cause leaks, internal water damage to the property and premature aging to the roof. Regular roof inspections and clearing of debris is an economical way to prevent expensive emergency callouts for leaks and premature roof replacements.
