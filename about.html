About Us

A FAMILY OWNED AND OPERATED BUSINESS

Owner of TP Roofing, Trent Faulkner personally provides all assessments and quotations for roof repairs and maintenance. He is a certified and licensed Roof Tiler and Roof Plumber, and with over 25 years’ experience in the roofing industry. Trent’s extensive knowledge and experience in roof installations, roof repairs and roof maintenance guarantees you will receive expert advice and quality workmanship for your property.

Trent is renowned in the industry for his honesty, integrity, hard work and experience in the field. He sources roof leaks and clearly outlines what needs to be done to fix the roof at a specified price.

ROOF SAFETY

As members of the Housing Industry Association (HIA) and The Master Roof Tilers and Slaters Association, we are provided with invaluable support and up to date industry information to enable us to maintain our high safety standards when working from heights. Safety is always our highest priority. We undertake our work in accordance with SafeWork NSW regulations and ensure we comply with the NSW Occupational Health and Safety Act.

The safety and continued education of all our staff is vital.

Valid certificates of currency for Workers Compensation and Public Liability can be provided on request.
