TP Roofing thoroughly assess your roof problems and provide roof repairs and roof maintenance to tile, metal, slate and polycarbonate roofs. You receive prompt, reliable, personal service and roofing solutions at an affordable price by our licenced roof tilers and roof plumbers.

Roofing repair and regular roof maintenance extend the life of your existing roof.

Roof repair services include:

  • TILE ROOF REPAIR
  • SLATE ROOF REPAIR
  • METAL ROOF REPAIR
  • POLYCARBONATE ROOF

Regular roof inspections to check for any cracked tiles, displacement of tiles and clearing of debris from your roof and gutters is an economical way to prevent expensive emergency callouts for leaks and premature aging of your roof. Determine if your roof has sarking installed, if there is sufficient ventilation and adequate insulation in your ceiling space. Have your flashings, cement mortar bedding, gutters and downpipes checked by a qualified roofing specialist today.
